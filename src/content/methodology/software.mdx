On the software layer, decentralisation is evaluated by looking at the open source repositories associated with different blockchain clients, and specifically looking at their commit history. Specifically, the following metrics are presented in the dashboard: Shannon entropy, Herfindahl-Hirschman index (HHI), 1-concentration ratio, number of total entities.

For blockchains that are implemented by more than one independent client, the commit histories of all of their client repositories are merged into a single, chronologically ordered history before any metrics are calculated, so that decentralisation is evaluated at the level of the blockchain as a whole rather than per individual client. Note that Ethereum's execution and consensus clients are treated as two separate systems for this purpose.

In the <Term>Client Distribution</Term> part, decentralisation is also evaluated by looking at the distribution of the blockchain clients themselves. With the exception of <Chain ledger="cardano">Cardano</Chain>, the blockchain data that is needed to determine these distributions are collected using crawlers running on each network. The results are based only on data we have collected and do not include extensive historical data.

For the <Chain ledger="bitcoin">Bitcoin</Chain>, <Chain ledger="bitcoin_cash">Bitcoin Cash</Chain>, <Chain ledger="litecoin">Litecoin</Chain> and <Chain ledger="zcash">Zcash</Chain> networks, the crawler we use is based on the <AppLink href="https://bitnodes.io/">Bitnodes</AppLink> project.

In <Chain ledger="ethereum">Ethereum</Chain>'s case, the network architecture consists of two distinct layers: execution and consensus. The dashboard reflects this architectural separation. The crawler used is <AppLink href="https://github.com/cskiraly/fast-ethereum-crawler">fast-ethereum-crawler</AppLink>.

Finally, for <Chain ledger="cardano">Cardano</Chain>, the official documentation refers to only one client implementation, and the dashboard therefore displays it. Several other clients are in development, but not ready for production yet: <AppLink href="https://github.com/blinklabs-io/dingo">Dingo</AppLink>, <AppLink href="https://github.com/harmoniclabs/gerolamo">Gerolamo</AppLink>, <AppLink href="https://github.com/pragma-org/amaru">Amaru</AppLink>, <AppLink href="https://github.com/txpipe/dolos">Dolos</AppLink>.
